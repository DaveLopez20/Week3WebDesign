Zombieland

It is a story about a man named Columbus who is trying to survive the zombie apocalypse, and then he meets a man named Tallahassee since he is looking for some Twinkies. They would later meet two survivors called Wichita and Little Rock and soon realize they would need each other to survive the apocalypse. It's a good zombie movie if you aren't interested in the drama within the apocalypse and just wanted to have a good time

Meet the Robinsons

This is a animated film made by Disney. This is a story about Lewis an orphan boy who is an inventor and wanted to win the science fair. One Day one of his inventions went chaotic and made a mess in the gym. He was ready to give up until a boy from the future appeared and tell him to not give up but he didn't want to. So he decided to send him to the future to help him get ready and later on they would have adventures and all that. This movie is good to watch due to a good message and a really cool imagination on what would the future would look like if we have great scientisits.

Monty Python and the Holy Grail

This one of the classics on what you are watching. This movie is about King Arthur who is gathering knights in search of the holy grail. This movie is fantastic due to being one of the funniest movies of all time. It came out in 1975 and yet the jokes still land it goes to show that if your movie is good to this day that means it is a classic.
